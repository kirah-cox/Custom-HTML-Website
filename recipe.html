Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecans, chopped (optional)
  • 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% Cacao Baking Chips

Baking Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the butter with the sugar and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla, one at a time.
    Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually blend dry mixture into creamed mixure.
    Stir in nuts and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by the tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!
